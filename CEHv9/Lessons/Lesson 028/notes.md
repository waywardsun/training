#### Video 28 Notes

##### Port Discovery

---

- `nmap` Scan Types
  - `-sS` -  (TCP SYN scan)
  - `-sT` -  (TCP connect scan)
  - `-sY` -  (SCTP INIT scan)
  - `-sA` -  (TCP ACK scan)
  - `-sW` -  (TCP Window scan)
  - `-sM` -  (TCP Maimon scan)
  - `--scanflags` - (Custom TCP scan)
  - `-sZ` -  (SCTP COOKIE ECHO scan)
  - `-sO` -  (IP protocol scan)
  - `-b ` - <FTP relay host> (FTP bounce scan)
  - `-sX` -  Xmas scan
    - Sets the FIN, PSH, and URG flags, lighting the packet up like a Christmas tree.
